Iron man

Iron Man, a prominent figure in the Marvel Universe, has captivated audiences with his high-tech armor, quick wit, and unwavering determination. Created by the legendary Stan Lee, Larry Lieber, Don Heck, and Jack Kirby, Iron Man made his first appearance in "Tales of Suspense" #39 in 1963. Since then, this iconic superhero has become a symbol of resilience and innovation.
